Method

Effective isotropic radiated power is EIRP = Ptx + Gtx. Received signal strength is RSS = EIRP + Grx − Lpath − Lmisc, where Lpath is either your entered path loss or a computed free-space path loss.

When calculating from distance, free-space path loss uses FSPL(dB) = 20·log₁₀(d_km) + 20·log₁₀(f_MHz) + 32.44 — the standard model for line-of-sight propagation with no obstructions. Real-world paths with obstructions, terrain, or foliage will show higher loss than this ideal figure.

Link margin is RSS − sensitivity. A positive margin of at least 10–15 dB is generally considered a comfortable working link; anything closer to 0 dB is marginal and vulnerable to fading or interference.
